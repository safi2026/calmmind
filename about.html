Our Story

How CalmMind Therapy came to be — and where we are going

"Mental health is not a destination, but a process. It's about how you drive, not where you're going."
— Noam Shpancer, Clinical Psychologist

How It Started

CalmMind Therapy was founded in 2018 by a group of five licensed clinicians who shared a common frustration — mental health care in America was either prohibitively expensive, heavily stigmatized, or simply too difficult to access for the average person. Waitlists stretched for months. Insurance coverage was limited. And too many people were suffering in silence.

They rented a modest two-room office in San Jose, California, hung a simple sign, and opened their doors with one clear promise: no one who needs help should be turned away because of cost, fear, or stigma.

Within the first year, they had served over 80 clients. By 2020, demand had grown so significantly that they expanded to a full suite and began offering online sessions — a decision that proved prescient as the world navigated the mental health crisis brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic.

What We Stand For

The values that guide everything we do

🤝 Compassion First

We lead with empathy in every interaction. Every client is treated as a whole person — not a diagnosis, not a case number. We listen deeply before we speak, and we never minimize what you are going through.

🔒 Confidentiality Always

Trust is the foundation of effective therapy. Everything shared within our sessions stays within our sessions. Our therapists adhere strictly to HIPAA regulations and professional ethical codes, with no exceptions.

🔬 Evidence-Based Practice

We do not guess. Our therapists are trained in proven, research-backed approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), EMDR, mindfulness-based therapy, and solution-focused therapy.

🌱 Growth Without Judgment

We believe every person has the capacity to grow and heal. Our role is not to judge where you have been, but to help you get to where you want to go. Our space is safe, inclusive, and affirming for all backgrounds and identities.

♿ Accessibility & Inclusion

We offer sliding scale fees, online sessions, flexible scheduling, and multilingual therapists to ensure that cost, location, and language are never barriers to getting the help you need.

Why This Work Matters

The mental health crisis is real — and it demands a real response

According to the World Health Organization, 1 in 4 people globally will be affected by a mental health disorder at some point in their lives. In the United States alone, over 57 million adults live with a mental health condition — yet fewer than half receive any form of treatment.

The barriers are well documented: cost, stigma, long wait times, lack of insurance coverage, and geographic limitations. These are not abstract statistics — they represent real people, real families, and real suffering that too often goes unaddressed.

At CalmMind, we take these numbers personally. Every person we serve is one fewer person suffering alone. Every session is an act of resistance against a system that has historically undervalued mental health.
