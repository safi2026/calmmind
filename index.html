CalmMind Therapy

CalmMind Therapy was founded in 2018 with a single mission — to make professional mental health support accessible, compassionate, and free of stigma.

We believe every person deserves a safe space to heal. Our practice brings together licensed therapists who are committed to evidence-based care tailored to each individual.

Our vision is a world where seeking therapy is as natural as seeing a doctor — and where no one faces their struggles alone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often

If you are experiencing persistent stress, anxiety, sadness, relationship difficulties, or simply feel stuck, therapy can help. You do not need to be in crisis to benefit. Many clients come to us for personal growth and self-awareness. A good starting point is our Services page to find the right fit.

Your first session is an intake appointment where your therapist gets to know you — your background, current challenges, and goals. It is a relaxed, confidential conversation. There is no pressure to share more than you are comfortable with. By the end, you and your therapist will outline a plan together.

It depends on your goals and the nature of your concerns. Some clients see significant progress in 6–8 sessions. Others benefit from longer-term support over several months. Your therapist will regularly review your progress with you and adjust the plan as needed.

Yes. All our services are available both in-person and online via Zoom or Google Meet. Online sessions are just as effective as in-person therapy and offer greater flexibility. Simply select your preference when you book a session.

Session fees vary by service. Individual therapy starts at $120, couples therapy at $160, and family therapy at $200. We offer a sliding scale fee for clients who qualify. Full pricing details are available on our Services page. Contact us to discuss your options.

Absolutely. Confidentiality is the foundation of therapy. Everything discussed in sessions is strictly private. The only exceptions are situations required by law, such as imminent risk of harm to yourself or others. Please review our Privacy Policy for full details.

We require at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule a session. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee of up to 50% of the session cost. We understand emergencies happen — please contact us as early as possible at contact@calmmind.com if you need to cancel.

CalmMind Therapy is not an emergency service. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call 911 immediately. For mental health crises, contact the National Crisis Hotline by calling or texting 988. You can also visit your nearest emergency room.
