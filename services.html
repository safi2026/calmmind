Our Services

Evidence-based therapy tailored to your unique needs

Individual Therapy

Our one-on-one therapy sessions provide a confidential, judgment-free space where you can explore your thoughts, emotions, and behaviors with a licensed therapist.

We use evidence-based approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), mindfulness, and solution-focused therapy to help you overcome anxiety, depression, trauma, and life transitions.

  • 50-minute weekly sessions
  • In-person or online via Zoom
  • Starting at $120 per session
  • Sliding scale available

Couples Therapy

Relationships take work. Our couples therapy sessions help partners improve communication, rebuild trust, and develop deeper emotional intimacy in a safe environment.

Whether you are navigating conflict, recovering from betrayal, or simply wanting to strengthen your bond, our therapists will guide you both toward healthier patterns.

  • 75-minute sessions
  • In-person or online
  • Starting at $160 per session
  • Intensive weekend sessions available

Family Therapy

Family dynamics can be complex. Our family therapy sessions bring members together to address conflict, improve communication, and rebuild trust within the family unit.

We work with families navigating divorce, grief, parenting challenges, blended family dynamics, and adolescent behavioral issues.

  • 90-minute sessions
  • Up to 6 family members
  • Starting at $200 per session
